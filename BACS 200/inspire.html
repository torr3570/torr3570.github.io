Martin Luther King, Jr.

What makes a hero? A hero to me is someone who actually uses their power in having a voice to good use. Someone who stands up for themselves and for others. Someone who is truly fearless, or just really good at hiding it.

The heroes that I most admire have the following characteristics.

  • Social Responsibility - they are not quiet to the injustices around the world
  • Motivate others - people follow a great leader

Rosa Parks had such courage facing those who thought badly of people of color, and yet she came out on top. She fought for those people, and in the generation we are currently in we really should know about her. And, let's not forget our history so we don't make the same mistakes now in our current situations.
